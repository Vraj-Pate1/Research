Evolution Of The iPhone

iPhones have evolved so much over the years. From the first iPhone released in 2007, to the latest iPhone 12 of 2020. These are 6 of the most revolutionary iPhones that have been huge milestones for apple.

  • The iPhone (1st iPhone)
  • iPhone 4
  • iPhone 5, 5C, and 5S
  • iPhone 6, 6 plus, 6S, and 6S plus.
  • iPhone 7, and 7 plus
  • iPhone X

The iPhone

The iPhone that started it all. The first iPhone was unveiled by Steve Jobs on January 9, 2007 but was available to purchase in June. it was a simple design. It had 1 2 mega pixle camera on the back of the device with a small, 3.5 inch touch screen. The 4GB models started at $499 and the 8GB models started at $599.

The iPhone 4

Between the iPhone and iPhone 4 came the iPohne 3G and 3GS. They came with new features such as GPS, 3G network conectivity and more storage. But the iPhone 4 came with a redisigned body, with flat edges and rounded corners. With the new iphone arrived Facetime, the newly renamed IOS 4 and the first ever retina display on an iPhone. Jobs unveiled the new iphone on June 7, 2010 and it went on sale on June 24. iPhone 4 was cheeper and faster than the first iPhone. $199 for 16GB, $299 for 32 GB.

The iPhone 5, 5C, and 5S

The iPhone 5 had a similar body to the iPhone 4 but it was slimmer than the previous iPhone. It came with a bigger 4 inch screen. The iPhone 5 was also faster. It was unveiled on September 12, 2012 and available to purchase on September 21. $199 for 16GB, $299 for 32GB, and $399 for a new 64GB option. The iPhone 5C and 5S were unveiled a year later on September 20, 2013 and available to purchase on September 25, 2013. The iPhone 5C was just a regular iPhone 5 with a redisigned body available in numerous bright colors. The 5C was available for $99 for 16GB. The iPhone 5S came with a new homebutton, capable of Touch ID, a new way to unclock the iPhone. the iPhone 5S came with new motion data proccessors and a faster 64-bit A07 SoC.
