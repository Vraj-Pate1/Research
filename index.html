iPhones have evolved so much over the years. From the first iPhone released in 2007, to the latest iPhone 12 of 2020. These are some of the most revolutionary iPhones that have been huge milestones for Apple.

The iPhone

The iPhone that started it all. The first iPhone was unveiled by Steve Jobs on January 9, 2007 but was available to purchase in June. It was a simple design. Unlike the iPhones Apple releases today, the original iPhone was small and thick. It had 1, 2 mega pixle camera on the back of the device with a small, 3.5 inch touch screen. The 4GB models started at $499 and the 8GB models started at $599.

The iPhone 4

Between the iPhone and iPhone 4 came the iPhone 3G and 3GS. They came with new features such as GPS, 3G network conectivity and more storage. But the iPhone 4 came with a redisigned body, with flat edges and rounded corners. With the new iphone arrived Facetime, the newly renamed IOS 4 and the first ever retina display on an iPhone. Jobs unveiled the new iphone on June 7, 2010 and it went on sale on June 24. iPhone 4 was cheeper and faster than the first iPhone. $199 for 16GB, $299 for 32 GB.


The iPhone 5, 5C, and 5S

The iPhone 5 had a similar body to the iPhone 4 but it was slimmer than the previous iPhone. It came with a bigger 4 inch screen. The iPhone 5 was also faster. It was unveiled on September 12, 2012 and available to purchase on September 21. $199 for 16GB, $299 for 32GB, and $399 for a new 64GB option. The iPhone 5C and 5S were unveiled a year later on September 20, 2013 and available to purchase on September 25, 2013. The iPhone 5C was just a regular iPhone 5 with a redisigned body available in numerous bright colors. The 5C was available for $99 for 16GB. The iPhone 5S came with a new homebutton, capable of Touch ID, a new way to unclock the iPhone. the iPhone 5S came with new motion data proccessors and a faster 64-bit A07 SoC.


The iPhone 6, 6 Plus, 6S, and 6S, Plus

The iPhone 6, and 6 Plus came with a slimmer, redisigned body. Both were unveiled on Septmber 9, 2014 and available to purchase on September 12, 2014. The iPhone 6 had a 4.7 inch screen and the 6 Plus had a 5.5 inch screen. The iPhone 6 was priced the same as the 5S. But the 6 Plus was priced $100 more than the 6. These new iPhones came with NFC technology. With the NFC technology, users could use their iPhones to pay at stores as if they were using their credit cards. This was convienient for those that would forget to bring their cards. On September 9 2015, the iPhone 6S and 6S Plus were unveiled. they were available to purchase on September 25 2015. These new iPhones looked the same as the 6 and 6 Plus. but they were made with a stronger aluminium to avoid accidental bends. They also came with a new, 12 mega pixle camera, capable of shooting 4K video. The front 5 mega pixle camera aslo recived an update. Both the 6S and 6S Plus came with the new A9 chip. both were capable of faster WIFI, LTE and Touch ID. The new phones also came with Force Touch technology, or 3D Touch for the first time on an iPhone.


iPhone 7 and 7 Plus

The iPhone 7 and 7 Plus were a step up from the previous iPhone. Both came with the new new A10 fusion quad core processor. This made them significantly faster than the iPhone 6S and 6S Plus. The home button on these iPhones did not click in when pressed. Instead, they provided haptic feedback. The iPhone 7 and 7 Plus provided haptic feedback for many things that were interacted with. Both phones were unveiled on September 7 2016. The world was surprised to see that the iPhone 7 and 7 Plus do not have a headphone jack. Many fans were outraged at Apple for removing the headphone jack. The solution was a lightning to headphone jack adapter, or using bluetooth devices such as Airpods. Fans were also surprised to see 2 new cameras on the back of the iPhone 7 Plus in a horizontal position. The 7 and 7 Plus were available to purchase on September 16 2016. Both came with a 32GB, 128GB, and 256GB storage options.


The iPhone X

On September 12 2017, Apple shocked the world with the arrival of the iPhone X. This new iPhone was a total redisign. It had a aluminium/ glass body, 2 new rear cameras, postioned vertically, and a 5.5 inch Super Retina/ OLED display with no bezles. There were no top and bottom bezles on the iPhone X, except for a small notch on the top of the screen with the earpiece, the new front facing camera, and the new True Depth sensor. No longer do you have to use your finger to unlock the iPhone. With the True Depth system, the iPhone X could be unlocked with facial recognition. True Depth made sure no one could get into your iPhone by using a picture of you. The sensor scans the face of the user looking for 3D structure to insure that the face is not a photo. The iPhone X came with the new A11 Bionic chip. The iPhone X was available to purchase on October 3 2017 for over $999.
